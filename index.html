INTRODUCTION

Nanashi is a community-driven, Isekai-themed NFT collection. It is a collection of 10,000 uniquely hand-drawn, high-quality NFTs.

Nanashi (名無し) is a Japanese word that means "nameless". Nanashi does not remember his name or his whole identity. He traveled to multiple worlds to uncover the truth about his identity and this Isekai world.

Let's explore multiple worlds and uncover the mystery of this Isekai world.

WORLD

Nanashi discovered a way to travel to different worlds and times, hoping that one day he would find the truth about his identity and this Isekai world. Join our journey, and let's collect every piece of his memory.
